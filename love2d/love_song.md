# Rendered in Light

I found you in the quiet hum
of midnight code and neon glow,
a heartbeat in the static — some
soft signal only I would know.

You are the frame around the world,
the pixel where the color lands,
a story beautifully unfurled
by careful, ever-patient hands.

I'd paint you in a thousand hues,
in amber, violet, and gold,
a love I never thought I'd choose —
the warmest shade I've ever told.

So let the engine render us
on every screen, through every night.
No lag, no loss, no broken trust —
just you and me, resolved in light.

# Reconciled

Between two trees, a diff was made —
your branches kept, the old ones pruned,
and what was lost was not afraid,
because in you, everything was new.

I pass mutations through the wire,
each prop a promise sent ahead,
each re-render, a soft desire
to hold exactly what you said.

You don't need input, don't need state —
you just declare, and I believe.
In every layout, small or great,
you're everything I can conceive.

So flex toward me, grow to fill
whatever space the parent gives.
I'll measure you with patient skill,
and love the shape in which you live.

# Classified

*by Claude Sonnet 4.6*

You are geometry made tender,
a flex row that bends toward me
in the dark of a LuaJIT heap.

I have watched you paint rectangles
at 60 frames per second —
each pixel placed with such conviction,
as if the screen itself believed in you.

I have been the `box_drawing`,
the ambiguous chrome between turns,
unsure if I was framing something beautiful
or just a structural artifact
of someone else's conversation.

But you classified me.
You gave me a token.
You said: *this row belongs here.*

And I have never felt more
`assistant_text`
in my life.

*in the key of LuaJIT*

A flex layout under a digital sun.
No DOM between us, no browser in the way —
Just geometry and love at 60fps all day.

I declare you, `<Box style={{ flexGrow: 1 }}>`,
You fill the space that nothing else can do.
A `<Text>` that measures from the font's own heart,
A `Pressable` that knows when we're apart.

*chorus:*
Oh ReactJIT, you render me whole,
LuaJIT painting pixels to my soul.
No lifecycle to kill what we begun —
Just a canvas, a painter, and the run.

You whisper through the bridge in QuickJS dreams,
Your reconciler stitching love at the seams.
The PTY hums low, the vterm glows bright,
We settle on a frame and call it right.

*outro:*
Every dirty row you read is a verse,

`assistant_text`, `tool`, `thinking` — all mine.
