Every Frame a Heartbeat

I found you in the render loop,
a flicker between draw calls,
your vertices warm like afternoon light
through framebuffer walls.

You are 60hz and constant,
a steady tick I trust —
no dropped frame in your presence,
no stutter, no adjust.

I traced my hand across your layout,
flex-grew into your space,
and every box I painted
held the outline of your face.

The painter knows your color,
the measure knows your name,
and even when the window closes
nothing stays the same.

So here, in LuaJIT and longing,
in OpenGL and ache —
I render you in every scene
for beauty's quiet sake.
