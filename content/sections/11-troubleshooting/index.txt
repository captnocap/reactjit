=== OVERVIEW ===
This section covers the most common issues encountered when working with ReactJIT, their
causes, and how to fix them. If something looks wrong in your layout, a component is not
rendering, or events are not firing, start here.

Common errors covers the specific error messages and visual symptoms you are most likely
to encounter, with before/after code examples showing the fix.

The FAQ answers fundamental questions about the framework -- why there is no DOM, why
flexGrow instead of hardcoded heights, how to add new features, and other conceptual
topics that come up repeatedly.
